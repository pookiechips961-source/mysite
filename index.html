HILL CIPHER DEMONSTRATION:

About Hill Cipher

Above video demonstrates Hill cipher by multiplying the message, "ACT" (in the form of a matrix) by the key matrix. The resulting matrix when read in mod 26 gives us the ciphered message, "POH".

In order to decrypt "POH", we multiply it's matrix by the inverse of the key matrix and the result is the original message (ACT) matrix.
